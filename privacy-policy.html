Privacy Policy for JobFilter - Company Blocklist

Last updated: March 19, 2026

JobFilter helps users hide job postings from companies they do not want to see. This extension is designed to process data locally in the browser.

Information We Collect

JobFilter does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, financial information, health information, or authentication credentials.

Data Stored by the Extension

The extension stores only a user-managed blocklist of company names in chrome.storage.sync so the list can persist across sessions and, if enabled by Chrome, sync across the user’s signed-in browser profile.

How Data Is Used

Company names in the blocklist are used solely to hide matching job postings on supported pages (LinkedIn Jobs and Built In job listings).

Data Sharing

JobFilter does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties. The extension does not send blocklist data or page content to external servers.

Website Access

JobFilter requests host access to LinkedIn (https://www.linkedin.com/*, https://linkedin.com/*) and Built In (https://builtin.com/*, https://www.builtin.com/*, and regional https://*.builtin.com/*) to read company names on job pages and apply the user’s blocklist locally.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.

Contact

For privacy questions about JobFilter, please contact the publisher through the Chrome Web Store listing or the project repository.
